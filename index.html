    Kelbe's Roller-Coaster Enthusiast List for Wichita Natives

    Hello and thanks for visiting my page! My name is Kelbe Fox and I am a student working towards an associates in Web Development. I have studied HTML, CSS, PHP, and MySQL. This site is my final project for Javascript, and I chose my hobby of roller coasters as the subject matter.

    As a roller coaster enthusiast, it can be a little daunting to live in a city without its own amusement park. Unfortunately, this is exactly the situation in my hometown of Wichita KS. Fortunately, there are 3 different amusement parks we can easily access within 300 miles (varying between a 2-5 hour drive). Although none of them are huge venues, they each offer a fun and unique lineup of rides. Keep reading to see the list below.

    Amusement Parks in the Wichita Area:

    • Frontier City
    • Silver Dollar City
    • Worlds of Fun

    The links above connect to my lists of the roller coasters at each amusement park. I included basic stats like height, length, speed, etc. I also included some personal touches like a uniquely calculated "thrill score" (out of 10) based on those stats. Each ride also has a fun little quote based on how a passenger might react after riding. There's even an embedded video that showcases each roller coaster! At the bottom of each list, you can click a button to reveal which coaster is my personal favorite.

    Since I am a roller coaster enthusiast, the coasters are all I focused on with my pages above. Make no mistake though, each of these locations have a ton of different rides, entertainment, and even dining venues that you won't want to miss out on. Im also including links to each park's official website below so you can check them out.
